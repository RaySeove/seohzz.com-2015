在不能获取域名转移密码时，可以通过向ICANN投诉域名注册商以获取域名转移密码，前提是你是域名的所有者，同时域名所有者的邮箱也是你的。

投诉地址如下：

https://forms.icann.org/en/resources/compliance/complaints/transfer/form

域名转移投诉范文如下：

Hello

I bought a domain(此处填写投诉的域名) in (此处填写投诉域名的注册年份，如2015).

I decided to transfer my domain to other for the better service and lower renew price.

But the agent disappeared and dont provide the auth code to me.

I think Im the domain owner and I have the right of transfering my domain by free.

Pls ask 此处填写投诉域名当前的注册商 send the auth code of 此处填写投诉的域名 to its owner email and regulate the behavior of Chinese agents.

Many Thanks

此处填写投诉域名所有者，必须与域名whios信息中的所有者一致

其中投诉域名当前的注册商可通过查询域名whios信息来获取，如万网是HICHINA ZHICHENG TECHNOLOGY LTD，新网是XIN NET TECHNOLOGY CORPORATION，新网互联是Beijing Innovative Linkage Technology Ltd。
